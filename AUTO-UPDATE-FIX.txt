BALL KHO GAYI XI — AUTOMATIC APP UPDATE FIX

This build removes the need for every player to uninstall or clear app data.

What changes:
- A new service worker installs immediately.
- Old caches are deleted automatically.
- Open app windows are taken over automatically.
- The app reloads once after the new version activates.
- Vercel is instructed not to serve stale HTML, JavaScript, CSS or service-worker files.

Expected player experience:
1. Deploy this build once.
2. Players open the installed app while connected to the internet.
3. The app checks for the update and reloads once.
4. The new interface appears.

Some Android browsers may require closing and reopening the app once, but no uninstall or storage clearing should be needed.
